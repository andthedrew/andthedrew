## 🚀 Projects

### [FlowHub — Smart Rural Mobility](https://github.com/andthedrew) &nbsp; `🏆 3rd Place · European Hackathon 2026`

A multimodal mobility concept developed at the **5th European Hackathon in Paris**, organized in partnership with the **French Ministry of Ecological Transition**.

The challenge: improve mobility access in rural and low-density areas. Our team built FlowHub — a network of autonomous electric minibuses operating on demand, integrated with a digital platform connecting users to existing transit lines.

- Designed the end-to-end product concept: routing logic, demand-based scheduling, and intermodal connectors
- Built the business model, financial feasibility analysis, and go-to-market strategy
- Defined a pilot deployment strategy targeting the **Lozère/Margeride region** (Occitanie)
- Pitched to a professional jury combining technical depth with strategic framing

> *Stack: concept design, business modeling, pitch deck — collaborative team project*
